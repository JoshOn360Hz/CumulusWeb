About Cumulus

Cumulus is built entirely with SwiftUI, the modern declarative UI framework from Apple.

It integrates Apple WeatherKit for fast, accurate, and hyperlocal forecasts straight from the source.

Cumulus is fully optimized for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS, providing a seamless experience across all your Apple devices.

No accounts. No ads. No subscriptions. Just weather.

Cumulus is 100% free and always will be.

Built with SwiftUI

Lightweight, fluid, and native to Apple platforms.

Powered by WeatherKit

Real-time weather data from Apple’s official source.

Free Forever

No tracking, no fees, no compromises. Ever.

Privacy Policy

Cumulus puts your privacy first. Always.

We don’t collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device, just as it should.

There is no account. No syncing. No risk. Just weather.

No Tracking

Cumulus doesn’t use analytics, ads, or third-party tracking libraries.

Location Stays Local

Your location is only used to fetch weather and never leaves your device.

Nothing Linked to You

We don’t store accounts or identifiers. Your usage is completely anonymous.
